Our History

Anaheim Electronics, founded in the late Universal Century, specifically in UC 0084, emerged as a key player in the development of mobile suits. Originally a subsidiary of the powerful Federation, Anaheim was established to innovate and produce advanced mobile weaponry in response to escalating conflicts.

By UC 0093, the company gained prominence for its role in developing iconic mobile suits like the RX-93 ν Gundam and the RGM-89 Jegan.

Throughout the Universal Century, Anaheim's contributions significantly influenced the balance of power in the ongoing wars, marking its legacy in the Gundam universe as a vital force in mobile suit engineering and technology.
